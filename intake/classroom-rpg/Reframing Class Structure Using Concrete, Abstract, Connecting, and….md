🚀 Final Adjustments Before Full Implementation  
  
🔹 Incorporating Your Final Adjustments  
✅ Interdisciplinary Lens: Always include healthcare + one other discipline + a universal theme.  
✅ Structural Analysis Lens (“The Medium is the Message”) explicitly integrated.  
✅ Cognitive Engagement Lens: Meta-cognition check-ins added.

📌 Updated Class Structure With Clearer Section Names & Canvas Integration  
  
🔹 Each class follows a structured rhythm that aligns with Canvas trackable components.  
  

| Time | Section Name | Purpose & Macro Connections | Canvas Component |
| --------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 10-15 Min | Real-World Foundations (Work & Healthcare Applications) | 🏥💼 Introduces healthcare, another discipline, & a universal theme to ground the week’s ideas in real-world applications. | Discussion Board (Small Group or Whole Class Case Study) |
| 15 Min | Big Ideas in Context (Historical Anchor & Core Questions) | 🌍 Establishes historical context, philosophical or technological shift that influenced this movement. | Low-Stakes Written Reflection or Quick Quiz |
| 10 Min | Personal Lens (Daily Journal & Meta-Reflection) | ✍ Connects ideas to personal experience & cognitive processing. | Ungraded Journal (Private Submission, Self-Assessment Form) |
| 20 Min | The Medium is the Message (How Literary Forms & Technologies Shape Understanding) | 📖🔊 Explores how inventions (printing press, radio, film, internet) altered the way we understand stories & meaning. | Canvas Page (Multimedia Embedded Lecture + Student Comment Section) |
| 10 Min | Building the Literary Toolkit (Key Terms & Narrative Structures) | 🏗️ Introduces key literary terms, forms, and structures that shape this movement. | Canvas Glossary (Students Add Examples Weekly) |
| 15 Min | Storytelling Across Mediums (Visual Art, Film, and Cross-Disciplinary Storytelling) | 🎨🎬 Compares how literature interacts with other mediums (art, film, digital storytelling). | Multimodal Discussion (Students Choose & Analyze a Piece of Media) |
| 20 Min | Bridging Ideas (Applying Past Concepts to New Readings & Vice Versa) | 🔄 Ensures literature feels like a continuum, not isolated texts. | Canvas Assignment: Compare & Contrast Chart (Past Movement vs. This Week’s Theme) |
| 5 Min | Sharpening the Craft (Proactive Writing Tip of the Week) | ✍ Provides quick, actionable writing advice students apply immediately. | Canvas Writing Resource Page (Students Comment With Examples) |
| 30 Min | Deep Dive: Guided Close Reading | 📖 Structured annotation, discussion, and inquiry into texts. | Canvas Annotations or Live Discussion Submission (Trackable Close Reading Work) |
| 15 Min | Looking Forward (Preparing for Homework & Upcoming Readings) | 📖 Ensures students know expectations, reading strategies, & next steps. | Assignment Prep Guide (Checkpoints for Upcoming Work) |
| 40 Min | Independent Inquiry & Research Task | 🔍 📝 Scaffolded low-stakes research, writing, & peer work leading to the final paper. | Weekly Mini-Research Task (Students Submit Notes & Findings) |
  
  
📌 Why This Works  
  
✅ Clear section names make the structure intuitive and easy to follow.  
✅ Healthcare + One Other Field + Universal Connection is now standard.  
✅ “The Medium is the Message” ensures students engage with how media forms shaped storytelling.  
✅ Canvas Integration ensures every section has a trackable task, making student progress visible.  
✅ Literary terms & structural analysis are built in without overwhelming students.  
✅ Meta-cognition check-ins help students track their learning.
